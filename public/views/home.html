On TheNewStudent users are members of classes. These groups share homework, and a private area to chat with peers. Teachers who create the class can manage the class, and set homework for the students, as well as help students who're struggling with questions.
Homework tasks are a set of questions either chosen by subject, or randomly by the application. They will be of a variety of forms and by completing them students will gain points. Students can also create their own private question sets to practice at home.
By using the chat functionality, students can relax with their peers, while discussing their work and school. The chat is realtime and volatile also allowing file uploads and sharing, meaning messages won't be saved, but appointed moderators can remove posts which are inappropriate.
